UX Flow

Scenario: Mary wants to order a custom cake for her friend 's graduation. She uses the bakery 's mobile app to select the cake 's size, flavor, and decoration, and enters her payment information. She gets an order confirmation and the cake is delivered on time and looks great.

Deliver
Feature Highlight
01
Select your preferences
By offering personalized cake recommendations based on users’ taste preferences, making it easy to find the perfect cake for them.
Pain Point
"It 's frustrating to search for a cake that fits my taste, and I don 't want to waste time or money on something I won 't enjoy."
02
Customizable Design

Designing custom cakes is easy and offers various options, making it simple to create people’s ideal cakes.

Pain Point
“I love having the ability to design my own custom cake, but it can be overwhelming to choose from all the available options.”
03
Add extra features
Beyond customizing the user 's cake, our platform allows users to personalize the entire cake experience to their taste, including adding a personalized card, choosing a candle design, and selecting the cake size before it arrives.
Pain Point
“I always felt frustrated that I couldn 't fully customize my cake and the overall cake experience.”
04
Track your order

By providing real-time updates on the status of a user 's cake order, making it is easy to track and monitor its progress until it arrives.

Pain Point
“I always worry about when my cake will arrive, and it can be stressful not knowing the status of my order.”
Reflection
Importance of iterations based on the user 's voice

Since we are not users, we have realized that we must continue to modify the design through user testing before deciding on the final outcome.
